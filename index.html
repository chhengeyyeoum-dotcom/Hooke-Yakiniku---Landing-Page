私たちについて — Our Story

About Us

Learn more about Hokke Yakiniku and our commitment to authentic Japanese BBQ. Every table is fitted with a binchotan charcoal grill, the same method used in Osaka's yakiniku houses for decades.

We source directly from trusted farms for our wagyu, and hand-mix every tare and marinade in-house, daily, without shortcuts.
